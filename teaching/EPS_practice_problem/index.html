Practice problem on EPS (examinable)

Past-year exam problem (adapted).

(Monetary amounts are in dollars unless otherwise specified).

Cheong Co reports a net income of 698791 for the year ended December 31 2020.

At the beginning of the same year (1/1/2020), Cheong Co has 3 million ordinary shares in issue and 1.3 million treasury shares (ordinary). The share price on 1 Jan 2020 is 0.87. Cheong Co also has 1.2 million 6% non-cumulative preference shares at 1/1/2020 with a nominal value of 1.2 per preference share (used to calculate the dividend). Each preference share is convertible at the holder's option into 1 ordinary share(s) at a fixed conversion date in 2025.

On 1 Feb 2020, Cheong Co reissues 61000 treasury shares as full payment for a property with fair value on this date of 54290. The transaction is an arms-length one under fair market conditions.

On 1 Mar 2020, Cheong Co reissues 88908 treasury shares at the market price of 0.88 per share.

On 1 Apr 2020, Cheong Co conducts a reverse split of its ordinary shares, replacing every 3 original ordinary shares with 2 new ordinary shares. The share price just before the reverse split is 0.86. The reverse split also applies to treasury shares.

On 1 May 2020, Cheong Co conducts a rights issue and 50000 rights to buy one share are exercised on the same day at an exercise price of 1.20. On 1 May 2020, the share price is 1.30 just before the rights exercise.

On 1 Jun 2020, Cheong Co reissues 33000 treasury shares as partial payment for a property whose fair value cannot be reliably determined. The share price is 1.35 on this date. The transaction is an arms-length one under fair market conditions.

On 1 Dec 2020, Cheong Co declares and pays a dividend of 0.01 per ordinary share, after meeting any prior requirements. The dividend can be received either in cash or in shares with the number of shares determined by the market value on that date. Holders of 136000 shares opt to receive the dividend in shares. The share price is 1.36 on this date.

On the balance-sheet date of 31 Dec 2020, Cheong Co has the following potential shares (potentially dilutive or antidilutive instruments) outstanding in addition to the convertible preference shares. All these have been outstanding throughout the year.

  • 8 million employee stock options vesting in 2023 with a strike price of 0.5. Each option gives the right to buy 1 share at the strike price.
  • Convertible bonds with a face value of 1 million dollars convertible to ordinary shares in 2023 in the ratio of 1 ordinary share(s) per 1 dollar of face value. The liability portion of the convertible bond had a carrying amount of 1009825 at the beginning of 2020. The effective interest rate on the convertible bonds is 3% annually compounded.

Other information:

  • Assume that the share price does not change between the dates mentioned in the question.
  • Cheong Co faces a corporate income tax rate of 17%.

Required:

Compute the basic earnings per ordinary share and the diluted earnings per ordinary share for Cheong Co for the year ended 31 Dec 2020. Compute annual averages over months not days. Show workings.
